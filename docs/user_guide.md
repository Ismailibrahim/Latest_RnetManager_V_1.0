# Rent V2 – User Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to **Rent V2**, your modern solution for managing rental properties, tenants, and units. This guide walks you through the core features of the application and shows you how to perform everyday tasks.

---

## Getting Started
1. **Access the app** – Open your web browser and navigate to the URL provided by your administrator (e.g., `https://rent.example.com`).
2. **Log in** – Use the email and password you received. If you forget your password, click **Forgot Password** to reset it.
3. **Dashboard** – After logging in, you’ll land on the dashboard, which gives you a quick overview of:
   - Total units
   - Occupied vs. vacant units
   - Recent tenant activity

---

## Core Features
### 1. Units Management
- **View Units** – Click **Units** in the sidebar to see a list of all units.
- **Add a Unit** – Press the **+ New Unit** button, fill in the details (address, rent amount, status), and click **Save**.
- **Edit a Unit** – Click the pencil icon next to a unit, modify the fields, and save.
- **Delete a Unit** – Use the trash icon; you’ll be asked to confirm the deletion.

### 2. Tenants Management
- **Tenant List** – Access via the **Tenants** tab.
- **Add Tenant** – Click **+ New Tenant**, provide name, contact info, lease dates, and assign a unit.
- **Upload Documents** – On the tenant detail page, use the **Upload Documents** button to attach lease agreements, IDs, etc. The system validates file types and size.

### 3. Payments
- **Record a Payment** – Open a tenant’s profile, click **Add Payment**, enter amount, date, and method.
- **View Payment History** – A table shows all past payments with filters for date range.

---

## Using the Units Page (Example)
1. Navigate to **Units** from the sidebar.
2. The page displays a table with columns: **Unit ID**, **Address**, **Rent**, **Status**, and **Actions**.
3. Use the **Search** bar to quickly find a unit by address or ID.
4. Click the **Details** button to see a full view, including current tenant and payment history.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- **Can I export data?**
  - Yes. Use the **Export** button on the Units or Tenants page to download a CSV file.
- **How do I reset my password?**
  - Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen and follow the email instructions.
- **What file types are allowed for document uploads?**
  - PDF, JPG, PNG, and DOCX up to 5 MB per file.
